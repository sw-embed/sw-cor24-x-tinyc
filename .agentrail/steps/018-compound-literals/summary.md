Fix compound-literal-as-local-decl-init stack-overlap bug. Root cause: gen_local_decl short-circuits for pre-populated locals (collect-pass outer vars) and doesn't bump locals_size — so a nested StmtExpr's on-demand allocations start at 0 instead of past the outer slot, causing the compound-literal temp to overlap. Fix in local_decl.rs: when var is in map, advance locals_size to -offset. demo60 now passes (61/64 demos, was 60/64). Parser-side compound-literal infrastructure (parse_compound_literal in expr.rs:383) was already in place; this was purely a codegen layout bug surfaced by the canary.
